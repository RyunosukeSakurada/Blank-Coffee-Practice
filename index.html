About Us

"Create a blank space in your hectic days"

Our concept is to provide a relaxing time and space for you, whose daily life is busy and your mind is tired from pushing yourself too much.


Blank coffee makes lavish use of a large site, and everything from the way the sun shines to the way it is built to block out the hustle and bustle of the surroundings has been thoughtfully designed.
